Welcome to All Sky Image Dataset

This gallery presents sky images captured continuously at the Maitri station and Bharati station in Antarctica, operated by the National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research (NCPOR). These images are part of ongoing atmospheric research efforts aimed at understanding upper atmospheric phenomena in polar environments.

Project Objectives

  • Monitoring auroral and airglow activities in the polar atmosphere
  • Studying mesospheric and thermospheric dynamics
  • Supporting atmospheric modelling and space weather studies

Instrument Details

  • Type: All Sky Camera (ASIM – All Sky Imaging System)
  • Field of View: 180° (fisheye lens)
  • Resolution: Up to 4K (varies by deployment)
  • Image Interval: Every 1–5 minutes (configurable)
  • Deployed at: Maitri Station, Antarctica
